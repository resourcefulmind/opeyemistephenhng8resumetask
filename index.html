About Me

I’m an enthusiastic Front-End engineer currently helping to shape the future of tech in Nigeria. I am strongly disposed towards learning about and creating user-centric, delightful and human experiences whenever people use tech. With a little over a year of experience, I have taken great delight in implementing products, first in my everyday learning projects and then in eCommerce, the Power Sector and as well as helping individuals get visibility for themselves and their businesses. I am a self-taught developer who has interned at a couple of firms but mostly works freelance, where I currently implement intuitive interfaces, interactions and experiences at the same time having fun in the process. I do like to share and discuss ideas and concepts which leads us to learn more. We should ask more questions to develop ourselves in terms of technology. I’m quite passionate about creating human centric products so my frontend work is driven by ease-of-use for the user. I believe in technology to create opportunities for people and businesses, especially in Africa. If you'd like to talk about a project you need help with, do shoot me a mail at omodaraopeyemi754@gmail.com . I'm currently AVAILABLE to work on fresh ideas.
